Instructions
To draw on the grid just click on a square and drag your mouse around. You can erase by clicking on an already filled square and dragging the mouse. If you'd like to erase the whole board just click the "Clear Drawing" button. When your drawing is complete click the "Guess Number" button and a neural network written with tensorflow will try to read your number. It is helpful to draw slowly as drawing quickly may skip over squares.
